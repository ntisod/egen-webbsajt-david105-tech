Hello.

Hello guys I am David and I am from Croatia. I like playing sports like: football, Handball, basketball, volleyball and etc.. As I kid growing up I trained football 6 years and then stopped. Because I realized football is not what I expected, I wasn’t happy. But then I discovered gaming and of course became addicted to it. Since then I dreamed to make games or play them at EA sports tournaments. But my parents like more idea of making games instead of competing. Unfortunately I was young and my grades weren’t so good in Croatia so I couldn’t achieve my dreams. But one day lucky for me, my parents decided that we need to move to some other country because Croatia didn’t make them happy. We decided to go too Sweden, because they heard it’s very good country, country of opportunities. When I moved out there my parents encourage me to start new page in life and become what I want to be. After a lot of thoughts I decided to study programming in “NTI Gymnasiet” in Södertälje. Finally I am happy with school after all those years!
